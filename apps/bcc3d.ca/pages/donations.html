Thanks for your interest in supporting the BC COVID-19 3D Printing Group. Your support is very important to us.

Demand for our products is increasing daily as more health care professionals run out of traditional personal protective equipment. We are rising to the challenge, but we can move faster with your support, either via materials or financial contributions.

Materials

We are in urgent need of clear plastics that we can form our face shields from. If you are aware of a source for PET-G, Laminating Sheets, Report Covers, Overhead Transparencies, or similar materials that might be suitable, our logistics team would love to hear from you. Please contact us directly with the supplier information.

Finances

As we ramp into larger scale production to support our provincial and local health authorities, financial support is key as we are resource bound, particularly when it comes to purchasing raw materials, and the cost of delivery of our products.

Every dollar counts, and we are grateful for any contribution you can allocate to our project. All funds received go directly toward production expenses.

If you would like to donate directly to our project, you can reach us at donations@bcc3d.ca. (Interac e-transfers are also accepted via this email address). If you'd prefer to support us via GoFundMe, you can find our page here.

Thank you!
