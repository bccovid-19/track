Request Ear Savers

Prolonged wearing of surgical masks or exam masks with elastic earloops can cause discomfort to the wearer's ears. This is currently being exacerbated by the limited supply of masks which is requiring many health care providers to wear the same mask for extended periods of time.

Ear savers are straps with hooks that go around the back of the head reduce pressure on the ears and associated discomfort to the wearer.

This item is provided in good faith as an emergency supply for use during a product shortage. This item has not been evaluated or authorized for use by any regulatory agency. Please use discretion when using this item and only use it if no authorized solution is available.
