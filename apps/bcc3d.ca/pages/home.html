Free personal protective equipment for British Columbia's healthcare providers and first responders.

BC Covid-19 3D Printing Group is a grassroots, volunteer-based organization dedicated to stemming the shortage of PPE for professionals who are serving on the front lines in the fight against COVID-19.

Our Current Offerings

3DVERKSTAN Face Shields

A proven face shield design that has been tested by medical personel all around the world. Each face shield consists of a frame and visor, both intended to be sterilized and reusable. Assembly is simple and takes only seconds.

3DV face shields are not currently approved by Health Canada. Please review our disclaimers below.

3DV Face Shield Specifications

Ear Savers (Surgical Mask Straps)

Surgical masks with elastic earloops can cause discomfort to the wearer's ears. These straps hold the elastics in place and greatly reduce the stress and discomfort associated with wearing masks for extended periods.
